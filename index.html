What in god's name is this?

This, my dear fellow, is the History of YWOT, as chronicled by a multitude of people and personalities.
We hope you enjoy this very very very much. Or, at least, a little bit.

But... why?

After being a member of the site for a month, Randomguy_ wanted to make learning about YWOT and the history therof easier for everybody. To do this, he made /ywotwiki (now /~YwotWiki), and also founded /historyofywot. These pages are an extention of Randomguy_'s "wiki" pages on YWOT.

How were the time periods divided?

They were mostly made around a year long, centered around a major event or occurence. Beta is self-explanatory. Early YWOT runs from the end of beta to when all features came out. Pre-Vsauce is just the time between that and the Vsauce Era, which began with Vsauce making YWOT famous. August 11th is just a random date, and spam wars runs until the update came out. This is the current era we're in.
